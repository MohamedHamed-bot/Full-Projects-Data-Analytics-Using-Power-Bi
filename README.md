## Project 1

# 👥 HR Analytics Dashboard - Full Insight Case Study
An end-to-end **Power BI** project designed to optimize workforce management by tracking employee performance, promotion eligibility, and retention metrics.

## 🎯 Project Objective
This dashboard provides HR managers with a data-driven tool to monitor a workforce of **1,470 employees**, focusing on **Retrenchment vs. Promotion** strategies and overall job satisfaction.

## 🚀 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
* **Total Workforce:** 1,470 employees (60% Male / 40% Female).
* **Retention Risk:** Identified **117 employees (8.0%)** due for retrenchment.
* **Growth Opportunities:** Highlighted **72 employees (4.90%)** eligible for immediate promotion.
* **Employee Health:** **92.0% Active Workers** with high job satisfaction ratings (84.6% High Rating).

---

## 🛠️ Technical Features & Navigation
I designed this dashboard with a seamless **Navigation System** to allow users to toggle between different views:
1. **Home:** Overview of general workforce demographics and service years.
2. **Action:** A focused list of employees categorized for promotion or retirement.
3. **Detail:** Deep-dive into job roles (Sales Executives, Research Scientists, etc.) and overtime analysis.

### 🔍 Analytical Depth
* **Service Years Distribution:** Analyzed seniority, revealing that the largest group has **10 years of service** (202 employees).
* **Commuting Analysis:** Categorized distance from office into "Very Close", "Close", and "Very Far" to understand proximity impact on retention.
* **Role-Based Analysis:** Detailed breakdown showing **Sales Executives (326 total)** as the largest department.
